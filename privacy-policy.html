Plain English summary: Pwani Stays collects basic contact and search information to help you find rentals on the Kenya Coast. We use Google Analytics and Google AdSense, which place cookies on your device. We do not sell your personal data. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us on WhatsApp.

1. Who We Are

Pwani Stays is a property listing platform connecting renters and Airbnb guests with landlords and hosts along the Kenya Coast, specifically in Mtwapa, Shanzu, Kikambala and surrounding areas.

We operate this website at pwanistays.co.ke. For the purposes of Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019, Pwani Stays is the Data Controller responsible for the personal data processed through this website.

2. Data We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Name and phone number (when you contact us via WhatsApp or a listing enquiry form)
  • Email address (if you sign up for alerts or a landlord account)
  • Property details (if you submit a listing as a landlord)
  • Search preferences (property type, location, budget — entered in the search form)

Information collected automatically

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, operating system, and device type
  • Pages visited, time spent on site, and referring URLs
  • Clicks on WhatsApp buttons and listing cards (event tracking)
  • Cookie identifiers placed by Google Analytics and Google AdSense (see Section 4)

Information we do NOT collect

  • Payment or Mpesa transaction details (payments happen directly between tenant and landlord)
  • National ID or passport numbers
  • Sensitive personal data as defined under the Data Protection Act 2019

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes, each supported by a lawful basis under the Data Protection Act 2019:

  • To connect renters with landlords — processing your search and displaying relevant listings (performance of a service)
  • To respond to enquiries — following up via WhatsApp if you contact us directly (legitimate interest / consent)
  • To improve the platform — analysing how visitors use the site to fix problems and add features (legitimate interest)
  • To serve relevant advertising — Google AdSense uses your browsing behaviour to show ads relevant to you (consent, managed via cookie banner)
  • To send updates — if you opt in to alerts, we may notify you of new listings matching your search (consent)

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal effects.

4. Cookies & Advertising

This website uses cookies — small text files stored on your device. We use three categories of cookies:

Essential cookies

Required for the site to function (e.g. remembering your search filters). These cannot be switched off.

Analytics cookies (Google Analytics / Firebase)

Google Analytics collects anonymised data about how visitors use this site, including which pages are visited and how long users stay. This data is sent to and stored by Google on servers that may be outside Kenya. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy. You can opt out using the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Advertising cookies (Google AdSense)

This site displays ads served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting google.com/settings/ads or aboutads.info.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site. The ads you see are not controlled by Pwani Stays — they are selected by Google's automated systems.

5. Third Parties We Share Data With

  • Google LLC — Analytics, AdSense, and Firebase (hosting and analytics). Google may process data outside Kenya. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • WhatsApp / Meta — When you click a WhatsApp button, you leave our site and your message is handled under WhatsApp's own privacy policy. We do not receive or store data from WhatsApp conversations.
  • Landlords and hosts — If you enquire about a property, your contact details (typically your WhatsApp number) may be shared with the landlord so they can respond to you. You initiate this contact.

We do not sell, rent or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights Under Kenyan Law

Under Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019 (and, where applicable, the GDPR for EU visitors), you have the following rights:

  • Right of access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — Ask us to correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to erasure — Request deletion of your personal data where we have no legal obligation to retain it
  • Right to object — Object to processing based on legitimate interest, including direct marketing
  • Right to withdraw consent — Where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting prior processing
  • Right to lodge a complaint — You may complain to Kenya's Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 10.

7. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Landlord listing data — Retained while the listing is active, and for 12 months after it is deactivated, in case of disputes
  • Enquiry messages — Retained for 6 months, then deleted
  • Analytics data — Google Analytics retains data for 14 months by default (configurable)
  • Email alert sign-ups — Retained until you unsubscribe

After these periods, data is permanently deleted or anonymised so it can no longer identify you.

8. Children's Privacy

Pwani Stays is not directed at children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in the law, our services, or Google's requirements. When we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Continued use of Pwani Stays after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
