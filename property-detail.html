About this property

Creekview Garden Apartment is a bright, airy two-bedroom unit sitting right along the lush Mtwapa Creek corridor. The apartment opens onto a private garden terrace where you can watch dhows drift past in the early morning light — a genuinely rare find on the coast.

The living space is fully furnished with a kitchenette, a comfortable sofa set and a ceiling fan in each room. The kitchen is equipped for self-catering; nearby shops and mama mbogas are a 5-minute walk. Both bedrooms face the garden, keeping the rooms cool through natural ventilation even in the hottest months.

Perfect for couples, small families or solo travellers wanting a quiet alternative to Mombasa's busier hotel strip. The host responds via WhatsApp within the hour.
