Why we exist

The problem we solved

If you have ever tried to find a house to rent in Mtwapa, you know the drill. You join a WhatsApp group. Half the listings are months old. You call a number — it is an agent asking for a viewing fee before you have even seen the gate. You find something promising on Facebook, send a deposit, and the landlord disappears.

Pwani Stays was built to cut through all of that. Every listing goes through a manual verification step before it goes live. We confirm the property is real, the landlord is reachable, and the details match what is advertised. No ghosts. No fakes.

From there, it is simple: tenants contact landlords directly on WhatsApp. No middleman. No commission taken from either side. The deal happens between the two people it should happen between.

We started with Mtwapa, Shanzu and Kikambala because that is the stretch of coast we know best — where hotel workers, teachers, NGO staff, and visitors are constantly looking for somewhere good to stay. We plan to grow, but we will not sacrifice verification quality to do it fast.

What drives us

What we stand for

✅
Verification over volume
We would rather have six real, confirmed listings than sixty unverified ones. Every property on Pwani Stays has been checked. That is the only promise that matters in this market.
🤝
No extraction, no friction
Renters pay nothing to use this platform. Landlords get a free 30-day trial. We charge only for premium placement — and that is optional. The transaction always stays between the two people involved.
🔍
Transparency by default
Prices are shown upfront. Amenities are listed honestly. If a property has a known issue or limitation, we say so. We are more useful to you as a source of truth than as a sales channel.
🚀
Built to grow with landlords
Many landlords on the Coast are renting for the first time and do not know how to present their property online. We help with that — photos, descriptions, pricing guidance — so listings actually convert.
🏠
Community first
Mtwapa, Shanzu and Kikambala are not just markets to us. They are communities with real people looking for stable housing and fair deals. We want to make those deals easier and safer.
📱
Mobile-first, coast-aware
Most people browsing here are on a phone, possibly with variable data. We design for that — fast pages, WhatsApp-first contact, and no unnecessary steps between you and a landlord.
Where we operate

The areas we know

We focus on three neighbouring areas along the Kenya North Coast — each with its own character, price range, and community.

Mtwapa
Kilifi County · Most listings
The commercial heart of the North Coast. Good transport links to Mombasa, a busy town centre, and a wide range of rental options from bedsitters to furnished 2-bedrooms. Popular with hotel and hospitality workers.

Shanzu
Kilifi County · Ocean access
A quieter, more residential area with ocean access and a growing number of holiday lets and Airbnbs. Favoured by expats, remote workers, and tourists wanting a more laid-back base than central Mombasa.

Kikambala
Kilifi County · Beach cottages
Further north and more exclusive. Known for beach cottages, resort-adjacent stays, and a peaceful environment away from town noise. Suits visitors and longer holiday rentals more than short-stay workers.
