Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting and listing on Pwani Stays.

For Renters

For Questions

Is there an agent fee for renters?
No. Pwani Stays charges zero fees to renters. You contact the landlord directly via WhatsApp. Any arrangement is agreed directly between you and the landlord.
How do I know a listing is real?
Every listing with a Verified badge has been physically visited and confirmed by our team. We check the property exists, matches the description, and the landlord is contactable.
How do payments work?
Payments are made directly to the landlord via Mpesa. Pwani Stays does not handle any money. Always confirm details via phone or in person before sending any deposit.
Can I view a property before paying?
Yes, and we strongly recommend it. Arrange a viewing via WhatsApp before paying any deposit. Legitimate landlords will always allow a viewing.
What if I find a fake listing?
Report it immediately via our Contact page or WhatsApp. We investigate all reports within 24 hours and remove fraudulent listings immediately.
For Landlords

For Questions

How much does it cost to list?
The first 30 days are free. After that: Basic is free, Standard is Ksh 700/month (WhatsApp button, gallery), Featured is Ksh 1,500/month (top placement, gold badge). See the full pricing page.
What is the Verified Badge?
A one-time Ksh 2,500 service. We visit your property, confirm it and photograph it, then add a verified tick to your listing. Verified listings get significantly more enquiries.
How quickly will my listing go live?
Standard listings go live within 24 hours. Verified badge listings take 48-72 hours to allow for our site visit.
Can I edit my listing after it goes live?
Yes. WhatsApp us with the changes needed and we will update your listing within a few hours.
What payment methods do you accept?
Mpesa only. We send a payment request via WhatsApp when your free trial ends.
